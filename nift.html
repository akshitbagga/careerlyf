About NIFT

  • Full Form: National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)
  • Established: 1986
  • Status: Statutory Institute (Act of Parliament, 2006)
  • Focus: Design, Technology, and Management for fashion + textiles industry
  • Campuses: Network of multiple campuses across India (e.g., Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata, etc.)

Admission Process

Admission is through a national-level entrance process (typically CAT + GAT, followed by Situation Test or GD/PI depending on program).

1

Written Exam

  • CAT: Creative Ability Test (drawing, observation, design ability)
  • GAT: Quant, Communication, English, Analytical, GK/Current Affairs
2

Second Stage

  • B.Des: Situation Test (hands-on material manipulation / model-making)
  • PG + B.F.Tech: Group Discussion + Personal Interview (GD/PI)
