About NID

  • Full Form: National Institute of Design (NID)
  • Established: 1961 (Ahmedabad)
  • Status: Statutory institute under DPIIT, Ministry of Commerce & Industry
  • Institution of National Importance: NID Act 2014
  • Mandate: World-class design education, research, and design awareness

Admission Process: NID DAT

Admission is based on the two-stage national-level Design Aptitude Test (DAT).

1

NID DAT Prelims

  • Pen-and-paper aptitude test
  • Assesses knowledge, comprehension, analytical & design skills
  • Must qualify to be shortlisted for Mains
2

NID DAT Mains

  • Typically includes a Studio Test (hands-on practical task)
  • May include a Personal Interview (for some programs)
  • Final merit is prepared based on DAT Mains score (Prelims qualification is mandatory)
