About UCEED

  • Full Form: Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design
  • Purpose: Admission to B.Des (Bachelor of Design)
  • Conducting Body: IIT Bombay
  • Frequency: Once a year (usually January)
  • Language: English
  • Total Marks: 300
  • Duration: 3 hours (Part A: 2 hrs, Part B: 1 hr)

Eligibility Criteria

A candidate must meet all conditions (age + class 12 timeline + attempts).

  • Age Limit: OPEN/EWS/OBC-NCL born on or after Oct 1, 2001
  • Age Relaxation: SC/ST/PwD born on or after Oct 1, 1996
  • Qualifying Exam: Class 12 first attempt in the exam year or the preceding year
  • Stream: Any stream (Science/Commerce/Arts) eligible
  • Attempts: Maximum 2 attempts in consecutive years

UCEED Exam Pattern

The paper is divided into two compulsory parts: Part A (CBT) and Part B (Offline Drawing/Design).

Part A tests aptitude in reasoning, analytical skills, visual ability, and general awareness.

UCEED Syllabus

The syllabus covers both analytical and creative aspects essential for a design career.

  • Visualisation and Spatial Ability: Transforming 2D shapes into 3D objects and understanding spatial relationships.
  • Observation and Design Sensitivity: Attention to detail, analysis, prediction, subtle properties of everyday life.
  • Environmental and Social Awareness: Awareness of environmental, social, and cultural connections with design.
  • Analytical and Logical Reasoning: Analyzing qualitative and quantitative information.
  • Language and Creativity: English proficiency, verbal/non-verbal analogies, metaphors, symbols.
  • Practical and Scientific Knowledge: Basic scientific principles and everyday object knowledge.
  • Drawing: Drawing products/people/scenes in proportion with good line quality, composition, perspective, shading.
  • Design Aptitude: Practical response to problems/situations with ingenuity and empathy.

Participating & Result-Sharing Institutes

UCEED score is a primary criterion for B.Des admissions in top institutions.

Participating Institutes (Joint Admission Process)

  • IIT Bombay
  • IIT Delhi
  • IIT Guwahati
  • IIT Hyderabad
  • IIT Indore
  • IIT Roorkee
  • IIITDM Jabalpur

Result-Sharing Institutes

Many other universities and private colleges accept UCEED scores, but candidates apply directly (not via IIT Bombay Joint Admission).

UCEED Test Outline

Quick snapshot of how the exam is structured:

  • Part A (CBT): NAT + MSQ + MCQ (Total 200 marks)
  • Part B (Offline): Drawing + design aptitude (Total 100 marks)
  • Total: 300 marks, 3 hours
